Fake news is a type of unprofessional journalism or propaganda. It is used to deliberately mislead the audience spread through traditional print and broadcast news media or online social media. Fake news is written and published with the intent to mislead in order to damage an agency, entity or person or benefit politically or financially. Fake News article often use techniques to make the article seem more interesting and get more people to increase readership and spread mis-information.
