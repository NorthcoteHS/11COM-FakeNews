Solving the issue of fake news.

When it was created, fake news were usually looked at with a less serious tone, as the news were often far away from the truth and recognizable with common sense. However, the current use of it to fool the public is a serious issue, and it needs to be solved for the benefit of all the stakeholders.

Some possible solutions

Solution Description Advantages Disadvantages
Fact checking The reader uses reliable websites that specializes in determining what is true and false by using careful research The reader are in control of what they choose to check, which saves time for all stakeholders. The fact that it is created by the community also means that it should be the least biased of all options. - There is still a possible bias in information provided
- The websites, while quite reliable, may not be correct at all times and cannot be looked at a completely reliable source
Police become main outlet of news The police creates news sites and other media outlets - Being an authority, they are a reliable source for news
- If they were to control all outlets it would make news viewing much easier and convenient		 - They can be biased, and they can use their power to manipulate the people much more easily
- The idea of complete control over media given to a party is dangerous
Shut down the Internet The internet is shut down, preventing any news being spread, untrue or otherwise - There is no online news, which means there would be no fake news, preventing any manipulation and misunderstanding - There is no news.
- Newspaper still exists, and fake news will be spread more widely there, rendering the solution useless.
Government fact checks The government controls and verifies the news that are brought out - The government are the most reliable source to verify and control the news (because they control everything)
- Functions mostly like with the police method, but to a larger scale		 - Effectively shuts down something called 'freedom of speech'
- Absolutely will be biased, as the most controversial news these days are about the government
- Takes us one step closer to this

Finally, looking through all the solutions, we can see that though imperfect, the best way to deal with this is through the use of fact-checking websites. Some possible sites to check for facts:
FactCheck.Org
Snopes.com
PolitiFact.com
OpenSecrets.Org
Alternatively, you can just Google 'Fact Check websites' for results.
