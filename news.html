Fake news swirling around the world today

When it was created, fake news were usually looked at with a less serious tone, as the news were often far away from the truth and recognizable with common sense. However, the current use of it to fool the public is a serious issue, and it needs to be solved for the benefit of all the stakeholders.

Some possible solutions

Solution Description Advantages Disadvantages
Fact checking Police become main outlet of news Shut down the Internet Government fact checks
The reader uses reliable websites that specializes in determining what is true and false by using careful research The police creates news sites and other media outlets The internet is shut down, preventing any news being spread, untrue or otherwise The government controls and verifies the news that are brought out
